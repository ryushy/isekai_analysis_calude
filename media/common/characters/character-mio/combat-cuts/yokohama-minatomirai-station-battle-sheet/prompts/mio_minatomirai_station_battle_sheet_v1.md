```text
Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: cinematic battle scene design sheet base image, later text overlay will be added separately
Primary request: Create a single high-quality visual design sheet for a battle scene between Mio Shinomiya and an encountered enemy inside a Yokohama Minato Mirai Line underground subway station concourse. Use the visible Mio light crimson functional turnaround as the character costume reference.

Important: Do not draw readable text, labels, numbers, logos, station names, route marks, ads, or watermark. Leave clean blank caption panels and dark translucent rectangles where text can be added later.

Subject and character: Mio Shinomiya, fictional 16-year-old Japanese kyudo heroine, same identity as the visible reference: rounded youthful face, soft cheeks, calm dark eyes, natural small mouth, airy bangs, glossy black long high ponytail, petite/slender build. Outfit: the visible light crimson functional archer costume, one-shoulder crimson sash, white chest wrap, black/deep-crimson obi, gold cords used sparingly, layered split red cloth panels, fitted black leggings/shorts, white forearm wraps, black fingerless gloves, practical black tabi-like boots. Keep her modest, non-sexualized, current-era, not final omnipotent form.

Enemy design: an encountered station anomaly, not human gore: a faceless smear-like enemy formed from black rainwater, ticket-gate shadows, torn commuter silhouettes, and reflective subway glass fragments. It has no clear single point at first, making it difficult for Mio. Add one tiny exposed weak marker/glint only in the later action panels.

Location: Yokohama Minato Mirai Line underground subway station interior at night: broad platform/concourse, glass safety doors, tiled floor with wet reflections, escalators, ticket gates, ceiling light strips, teal-blue and cold white station lighting, emergency red glow, distant tunnel darkness. No real station branding or readable signage.

Sheet layout: one wide 16:9 cinematic design board. Left third: full-body character/action pose of Mio in the light crimson outfit, plus a smaller enemy silhouette concept beside her. Right two-thirds: a 3x3 grid of cinematic storyboard frames showing a battle sequence. Around the edges, include empty clean dark panels and thin leader lines for later notes.

Storyboard visual beats, no numbers:
1 establishing empty underground station, wet floor reflections, enemy smear forming near ticket gates;
2 Mio notices from behind a pillar, low crouched tactical posture;
3 enemy spreads into many faceless overlapping positions, no single point;
4 Mio retreats along platform edge to regain distance, close-range pressure;
5 she braces behind a column or glass safety door frame and searches for a return marker;
6 a small glint/weak marker appears in the enemy mass;
7 Mio draws exactly one pale-gold light arrow, body face bow and target direction aligned;
8 short arrow glow strikes only the exposed marker, no continuous beam back to Mio;
9 zanshin after release, enemy collapsing into rainwater reflections, station lights flicker.

Cinematic requirements: premium anime production design with slight semi-real texture; strong depth; wet reflections; controlled teal/cold white/red emergency lighting; dynamic but readable action; consistent Mio costume and face across all appearances; enemy scary but non-gory; film storyboard feel.

Mio combat constraints: Current Mio, not final form. Range is limited and she needs a visible single point or marker. Show effort, concentration, and tactical repositioning. No omnipotent long-range certainty. No visible continuous beam that reveals her position. If arrow appears, show exactly one arrow state per panel; never show both nocked and in-flight arrow in the same panel.

Avoid: readable text, real station logos, ads, numbers, gore, guns, metal weapon emphasis, giant laser beams, magic circles, HUD, extra allies, duplicate Mio faces in the same panel beyond the storyboard structure, oversexualized design, changing outfit details, confusing body/bow/target alignment.
```
